## üí° Interview Tips

### Technical Questions
‚úÖ **Explain your reasoning** - Don't just write code, explain WHY  
‚úÖ **Discuss trade-offs** - "I used X because Y, but Z would be better if..."  
‚úÖ **Ask clarifying questions** - "Should this handle concurrent requests?"  
‚úÖ **Think out loud** - Share your thought process  
‚úÖ **Start simple, then optimize** - Get it working first

### Behavioral Questions
‚úÖ **Use STAR method** - Situation, Task, Action, Result  
‚úÖ **Be specific** - Use real examples from your experience  
‚úÖ **Show growth** - Explain what you learned  
‚úÖ **Be honest** - It's okay to say "I don't know, but I'd research X"

### System Design
‚úÖ **Clarify requirements** - Ask about scale, constraints  
‚úÖ **Start with high-level** - Draw architecture first  
‚úÖ **Discuss bottlenecks** - Identify potential issues  
‚úÖ **Consider trade-offs** - CAP theorem, consistency vs availability
